VibeStack — Privacy Policy

Effective: February 2026

Overview

VibeStack is a fully offline network engineering toolkit built by VibeCMD LLC. Your privacy is simple: we collect nothing.

Data Collection

VibeStack does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever. The app performs all operations entirely on your device with zero network calls.

No Account Required

VibeStack does not require any account creation, login, or registration. There are no usernames, passwords, or email addresses involved.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics frameworks, tracking pixels, advertising SDKs, or third-party data collection tools. No usage data leaves your device.

No Network Access

VibeStack makes zero network requests. It does not connect to any servers, APIs, or external services. All subnet calculations, port lookups, Linux command references, cable data, and troubleshooting flows are stored locally within the app.

No Third-Party Services

VibeStack does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or data brokers.

Children's Privacy

VibeStack does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Since we collect no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
