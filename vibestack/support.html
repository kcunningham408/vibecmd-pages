Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does VibeStack need an internet connection?

A: No. Every tool in VibeStack works 100% offline. No internet connection is ever required.

Q: Do I need to create an account?

A: No. VibeStack requires no login, registration, or account of any kind. Just open the app and start working.

Q: What permissions does VibeStack need?

A: None. VibeStack requires zero device permissions — no camera, no location, no contacts, no network access.

Q: Is VibeStack free?

A: Yes. VibeStack is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions.

Q: Does VibeStack scan my network?

A: No. VibeStack is a reference and calculation toolkit only. It does not connect to, scan, or interact with any networks.

Included Tools

  • SubnetBuddy — CIDR calculator, VLSM planner, binary visualizer, supernetting, overlap checker, IP analyzer, cheat sheet
  • PortBuddy — TCP/UDP port lookup for 400+ common ports
  • Linux QuickRef — 120+ essential Linux commands, searchable, tap-to-copy
  • CableRef — T568A/B pinouts, connector specs, fiber types, Ethernet standards
  • NetFlow — Interactive troubleshooting decision trees for 5 common scenarios
  • TimeCalc — Epoch converter, world time zones, duration calculator, cron decoder
  • ByteCalc — Byte/KB/MB/GB/TB converter, transfer time estimator
  • BaseConvert — Decimal, hex, octal, binary converter with network value reference
