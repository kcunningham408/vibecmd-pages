Common Questions

How do I log a glucose reading?

Go to the CGM tab and tap "Log Glucose Reading." Enter your value, trend, and optional notes.

How do I invite someone to my Care Circle?

Go to the Circle tab, tap "Invite Member," and share the generated code with your caregiver.

What are AI Insights?

Insights analyzes your glucose patterns to show trends, time-of-day patterns, variability, and actionable observations — all computed from your data, no external AI service needed.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Passwords are hashed, data is encrypted in transit, and we never sell your information.
