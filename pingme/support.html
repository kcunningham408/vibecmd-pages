Frequently Asked Questions

How does PingMe check my devices?

PingMe sends HTTP requests, TCP connections, or ICMP-like pings to your devices at the interval you configure. If a device doesn't respond within 10 seconds, it's marked as down.

How fast will I be notified when something goes down?

PingMe sends a push notification after 2 consecutive failed checks. On the free plan (5-minute intervals), you'll know within 10 minutes. On Pro/Unlimited (1-minute intervals), within 2 minutes.

Can I monitor local network devices?

PingMe monitors from our cloud servers, so the device must be accessible from the internet. For home lab monitoring behind a firewall, you'll need port forwarding or a reverse proxy.

How do I upgrade my plan?

Go to Settings in the app and tap Upgrade. Subscriptions are managed through your Apple App Store or Google Play account.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at vibesupport@vibecmd.net and we'll delete your account and all associated data within 48 hours.
