Privacy Policy

PingMe by VibeCMD LLC — Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and encrypted password. When you add devices to monitor, we store the device name, URL or IP address, and monitoring configuration you provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide uptime monitoring and send you alerts when your devices go down or recover.
  • To manage your account and subscription.
  • To send push notifications about device status (with your permission).

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas with encryption at rest. Check history is retained for 30 days on the free plan and 90 days on paid plans. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Push Notifications

We use Expo Push Notifications to send you device status alerts. You can disable push notifications at any time in the app settings. Push tokens are stored on our servers and used solely for delivering your alerts.

5. Third-Party Services

PingMe uses the following third-party services:

  • MongoDB Atlas — database hosting
  • Expo — push notifications
  • Render — server hosting

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at vibesupport@vibecmd.net.

7. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact VibeCMD LLC at vibesupport@vibecmd.net.
